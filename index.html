FAQ

Good questions, answered honestly.

How much does a website cost?

Every project is quoted individually based on scope. After a short discovery call, we send a fixed-price proposal — no surprise invoices, no hourly guesswork.

How long does a project take?

Most websites launch in 4–6 weeks from kickoff, depending on scope and how quickly we receive content and feedback. Brand identity and e-commerce builds may run longer — we'll give you a specific timeline before you sign anything.

Do I own the website when it's finished?

Yes. Once final payment is received, the site, its code, and its content are yours outright. We don't hold your website hostage on a proprietary platform or lock you into our hosting to keep it running.

What if I don't have photos, copy, or branding yet?

That's most clients. We offer copywriting and brand identity in-house, and can point you to trusted photographers in the Sarasota area — a blank slate is a starting point, not a roadblock.

Do you offer support after the site launches?

Yes. Every project includes a post-launch support window, and ongoing Website Care Plans are available for clients who want us to keep handling hosting, updates, and small edits long-term.

What platforms do you build on?

We choose the right platform for the job — hand-coded builds, Webflow, or Shopify for e-commerce — rather than forcing every client onto the same stack. We'll recommend the right fit during discovery.
